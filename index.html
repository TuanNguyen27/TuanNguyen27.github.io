About me


I am a junior at Swarthmore College majoring in Mathematics (with Statistics emphasis) and Computer Science. My CS interests are automation of data science and machine learning pipeline (autoML), dimensionality reduction, distributed optimization and missing data. I am also passionate about philosophy, photography and classical music.

This summer, I am doing research under Professor Steve Wang at Swarthmore College and Dr. Randal Olson at the University of Pennsylvania.

If you want to know what I'm thinking check out my blog.
